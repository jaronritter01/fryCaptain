About Us

I had the idea for the resturant while setting in another that had amazing french fries (Big Whiskeys) and across the table from me was my girlfriend. Now she loves french fries, so I look at her and I say,

"What if there was a resturant all about french fries. Would you eat there?".

And she said, "Heck yeah I would. That sounds amazing!"

And from that day on I've been trying to share our love of french fries to the rest of the world! The best way I could think to do that was to open Fry Captain's.
